An evolving portfolio. Exploring emotional storytelling, cultural insight, contemporary advertising, and identity systems.

01

Gymshark

AMAANI

Brand Experience

Problem

Showcase Gymshark as a sportswear brand, dedicated to the gym.

Insight

Gym-goers don't have time to think about their outfits, yet pressure to look good whilst working out is on the rise.

Idea

Using gym visual language to present Gymshark clothing as convenient workout wear.

02

Jaffa Cakes

AMAANI

Cultural Campaign

Problem

Gen Z don’t regard Jaffa Cakes as a popular, well-loved snack.

Insight

Doom-scrolling is a habit many fall into when it feels like your social battery has flatlined. Connected online yet disconnected in person.

Idea

Show that Jaffa Cakes is the solution to bring you back from your social slump.

03

Twinings

ARON

Social Connection

Problem

Tea brands continue to focus on heritage and tradition while young adults face a growing loneliness crisis.

Insight

Connection often begins with ritual. Tea naturally creates slow, intimate moments where people feel safe enough to open up.

Solution

An immersive experiential campaign transformed Twinings into a catalyst for connection through collaborative gameplay (in the world of Minecraft), physical touchpoints, and conversation-led interactions.

04

Wear With Flare

AMAANI

Awareness Strategy

Problem

Over 80% of people with psoriasis experience discrimination and humiliation because of their skin. This is often due to common misconceptions of their symptoms, such as being contagious.

Insight

83% psoriasis patients admitted to purposely concealing their symptoms to avoid more experiences of stigma.

Solution

Wear With Flare turning psoriasis from stigma to statement using social media campaigns and collaborations to encourage individuals with the condition to embrace their skin.

05

What Do You Call *****?

ARON

Digital Advocacy

Problem

Women’s health content is routinely censored online, while sexualised slang remains untouched.

Insight

Algorithms struggle to detect metaphor and coded language.

Solution

A culturally-driven language system that uses food metaphors to bypass moderation filters and keep conversations around women’s health alive.

06

NHS Breast Screening

AMAANI

Public Health Campaign

Problem

Around 1/3 of women invited for breast screening in the UK do not attend.

Insight

Many women take an 'ignorance is bliss' approach when it comes to their breast health.

Solution

Encouraging women to keep an eye on their breast health.

07

Pokémon Catchables

ARON

Product Concept

Problem

Traditional collectibles (such as Trading Card Game) often lose engagement once the unboxing experience ends.

Insight

Young audiences seek emotional interaction and self-expression through the things they collect.

Solution

Pokémon Catchables transformed collectibles into interactive companions through reusable physical eggs and connected digital experiences.
